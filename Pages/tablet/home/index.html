About Us

At Vio Sustainability, we partner with corporations and producer responsibility organizations to develop and implement effective strategies for waste and recycling collection working within extended producer responsibility mandates. We guide businesses toward improving capture and diversion rates, incorporating life cycle assessments, and implementing pilot projects with the goal of establishing a permanent system.


Our approach is grounded in data-driven methodologies, prioritizing stakeholder feedback and actionable insights. We support our clients with pilot project development, process analysis, benchmarking, and metrics-based implementation, ensuring they are equipped to make waste wise decisions that align with their sustainability and operational goals.

Projects

Recycle BC


Project - Responsible Supply Chain Project (Sept 2022 - Apr 2023


Recycle BC manages and collects all residential plastic, paper, and other materials in BC, in addition to ensuring they are properly recycled.

Button

Cleanfarms


Project - Northern BC Agricultural Plastic Collection Pilot (June 2023 - Current)


Under the Canadian Agricultural Strategic Priorities Program, Cleanfarms received funding to operate pilot programs in BC for the collection and recycling of agricultural plastics. Vio Sustainability is overseeing the operations and management of the pilot programs in the Peace River, Fraser-Fort George, and Bulkley-Nechako Regional Districts.

Button

Perk Eco


Project 1 - Coffee Cup Paper Recycling End Markets


Perk Eco is a recycling company and waste innovator that services cafes and coffee roasters. They are maturing the development of their proprietary method to fully recycle poly-coated paper cups and anticipate having significant amounts of rescued pulp from their process.

Perk Eco


Project 2 - Zero Waste Coffee Husk Pet Beds Consumer Market Study


Perk Eco produces circular economy products using coffee industry waste materials. In order to better understand their customer archetype, Vio Sustainability conducted a customer discovery project to pinpoint customer personas, shopping habits, material preferences, and willingness to spend.
